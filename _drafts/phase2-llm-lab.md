### Comparative Prompting Exercise

**Prompt**:  
> "Summarise the ethical challenges of deploying autonomous AI agents in healthcare."

**Instructions**:  
1. Run the prompt on at least **three different textâ€‘generation models**  
2. Capture and compare outputs for:  
   - Factual depth  
   - Ethical framing  
   - Clarity and tone  
   - Hallucination or bias  
3. Document findings in a markdown table  
4. Reflect on which model best aligns with the prompt intent and why
