# Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) Prompting Template

## Purpose
Combine external knowledge with generation for factual accuracy.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Context: [Provide retrieved text, document, or dataset]  
Output: [Answer grounded in context]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Summarise renewable energy adoption in India.  
Context: [Insert retrieved 2025 report excerpt]  
Output: A concise summary based on the provided report.
