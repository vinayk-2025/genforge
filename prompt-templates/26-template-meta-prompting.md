# Meta-Prompting Template

## Purpose
Guide the model to critique or improve its own output.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Directive: "Review your response and suggest improvements."  
Output: [Refined or corrected answer]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Summarise the causes of World War I.  
Directive: Review your response and rewrite it for clarity and accuracy.  
Output: Improved summary.
