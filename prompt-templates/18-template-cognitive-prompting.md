# Cognitive Prompting Template

## Purpose
Encourage deeper reasoning by asking the model to reflect on thought processes.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Directive: "Explain your reasoning process before giving the answer."  
Output: [Reasoning steps + final answer]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Decide whether the statement 'All birds can fly' is true.  
Directive: Explain your reasoning process before giving the answer.  
Output: Reasoning about exceptions (penguins, ostriches), then final conclusion.
