# Negative Prompting Template

## Purpose
Specify what the model should avoid in its output.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Constraints: "Do not include [undesired elements]."  
Output: [Answer without restricted content]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Write a short story about teamwork.  
Constraints: Do not use fantasy or magical elements.  
Output: A realistic story about a sports team.
