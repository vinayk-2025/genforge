# Thought-Source Prompting Template

## Purpose
Specify the perspective or source of reasoning the model should adopt.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Perspective: "Explain from the viewpoint of [role or source]"  
Output: [Answer shaped by perspective]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Explain the importance of teamwork.  
Perspective: From the viewpoint of a sports coach.  
Output: Explanation highlighting discipline, coordination, and shared goals.
