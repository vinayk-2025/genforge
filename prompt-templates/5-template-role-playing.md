# Role-Playing Prompting Template

## Purpose
Assign the model a persona or role to shape its responses.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Role: "Act as [persona]"  
Output: [Answer in role-specific tone and style]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Explain recursion.  
Role: Act as a computer science professor.  
Output: Explanation with academic tone and illustrative examples.
