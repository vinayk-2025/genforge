# Chain-of-Thought Prompting Template

## Purpose
Guide the model to reason step by step before giving the final answer.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the problem]  
Directive: "Think step by step."  
Output: [Final answer after reasoning]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Solve 12 + 23 * 2.  
Directive: Think step by step.  
Output: Show calculation steps, then provide the final result.
