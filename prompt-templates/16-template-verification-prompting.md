# Verification Prompting Template

## Purpose
Ask the model to check its own output against constraints or facts.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Directive: "Verify your answer against [constraints or facts]."  
Output: [Corrected or validated answer]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Provide the capital of France.  
Directive: Verify your answer against known geography facts.  
Output: Paris, confirmed as correct.
