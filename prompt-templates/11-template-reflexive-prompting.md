# Reflexive Prompting Template

## Purpose
Ask the model to reflect on its reasoning process after giving an answer.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the task]  
Directive: "Provide your answer, then reflect on how you reached it."  
Output: [Answer + reflection]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Solve 5 * (2 + 3).  
Directive: Provide your answer, then reflect on how you reached it.  
Output: Answer = 25. Reflection: Calculated inside parentheses first, then multiplied.
