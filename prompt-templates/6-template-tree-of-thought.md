# Tree-of-Thought Prompting Template

## Purpose
Encourage branching reasoning paths and selection of the best solution.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the problem]  
Directive: "List multiple possible approaches, evaluate each, then choose the best."  
Output: [Selected solution with reasoning]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Plan a study schedule for exams.  
Directive: Provide at least 3 approaches, evaluate pros/cons, then select one.  
Output: Final recommended schedule.
