# Self-Consistency Prompting Template

## Purpose
Generate multiple reasoning paths and select the most consistent answer.

## Template Structure
Instruction: [Describe the problem]  
Directive: "Provide multiple solutions and choose the most consistent."  
Output: [Final selected answer]

## Example Prompt
Instruction: Solve 15 + 3 * 2.  
Directive: Provide at least 3 reasoning paths, then select the consistent result.  
Output: Final answer after comparison.
